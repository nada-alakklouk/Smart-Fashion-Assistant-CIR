Category: Seasons
Title: Winter / Autumn
Content:
Outfits for the winter and autumn seasons usually look best in rich, deep, and heavily saturated colors that reflect the cool weather and cozy, layered aesthetics. Layered outfits, knitwear, coats, and heavier fabrics are also recommended to provide comfort while maintaining a polished seasonal look. For winter, vivid jewel tones and monochromatic blacks or whites are ideal, while for autumn, earthy shades like burnt orange, warm camel, mustard, and deep forest green are recommended because they enhance the warm dimension of seasonal wear. Layering with deeper tones creates a cozy and balanced look. Airy, chalky pastel tones and bright neon summer colors should generally be avoided, as they can look out of place against heavy fabrics and reduce the rich aesthetic of winter and autumn style.
Keywords:

ID: SS002
Category: Seasons
Title: Summer / Spring
Content:
Outfits for the summer and spring seasons usually look best in refreshing, lively, and lightweight color stories that mirror sunny skies and blooming nature. For spring, bright, warm tones like coral, sun yellow, peach, and clear apple green are ideal, while for summer, soft, cool-toned pastels such as powder blue, lavender, sage, and dusty pink are recommended because they keep the style refreshing and bright. Selecting lively shades creates a light and cheerful appearance. Heavy, solid blacks, dark charcoal greys, and muddy earth tones should generally be avoided, as they can absorb too much heat and reduce the lightweight feel typical of warm-weather style. Lightweight fabrics such as cotton or linen, breathable clothing, and comfortable footwear are recommended to stay cool while maintaining a fresh seasonal style.
Keywords:
summer season, spring season, lightweight fabrics, pastel colors, coral, sunny yellow, warm-weather style, lively shades, refreshing colors, summer, spring, warm weather, hot weather, lightweight, breathable, fresh colors, cotton, linen
